About ICASSSD

We are an independent international not-for-profit organization registered in Canada that envisions the advancement of research in the area of Systems Science and Sustainability in Canada and worldwide in alignment with the vision of the United Nations.

Headquartered in the Kitchener-Waterloo Region (the "Silicon Valley of Canada"), we conduct and utilize solutions in multi-disciplinary domains to make a real impact in communities worldwide.
