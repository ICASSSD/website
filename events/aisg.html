About the Series

The "AI for Social Good" seminar series brings together visionaries, researchers, and practitioners to discuss how Artificial Intelligence can be harnessed to address some of the world's most pressing challenges. From healthcare and education to environmental sustainability, we explore the transformative potential of AI when aligned with human values and social equity.
